Our Special Menu

Cappucino

A classic blend of rich espresso, steamed milk, and a velvety layer of foam. Perfectly balanced for a creamy and bold coffee experience.

Espresso Coffee

A strong, concentrated coffee shot with bold flavors and a smooth crema on top. Ideal for coffee lovers who appreciate pure intensity.

Cold Brew Coffee

Coffee steeped in cold water for hours, resulting in a smooth, less acidic drink. It’s naturally sweet and perfect for warm weather.

Iced Coffee

Freshly brewed coffee served over ice, often sweetened or combined with milk. A refreshing and versatile choice for coffee enthusiasts.

Frappuccino

A delightful blend of coffee, milk, ice, and sweet flavors, often topped with whipped cream. A dessert-like treat for coffee and sweet lovers alike!

Latte Coffee

A harmonious mix of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of foam. A milder, creamy option for those who enjoy a smooth coffee experience.
